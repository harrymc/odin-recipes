Unicorn Curry

A unicorn curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is popular in many parts of the world. It typically consists of tender chunks of unicorn that have been simmered in a rich and spicy sauce made from a blend of fragrant spices, herbs, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Unicorn meat
  • Onions
  • Curry powder
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coconut Milk
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Sear unicorn meat until browned on all sides
  2. Saute onions, garlic, and ginger in the same pot until tender and fragrant
  3. Add the unicorn meat back to the pot along with tomato paste, coconut milk, and curry powder
  4. Bring mixture back to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for at least 2 hours
  5. Enjoy with pita bread and rice
