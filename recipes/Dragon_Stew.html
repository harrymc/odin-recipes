Dragon Stew

A dragon stew is a comforting and hearty dish that is perfect for cold weather or when you're in the mood for some classic comfort food. It typically consists of tender chunks of dragon that have been slow-cooked in a rich, savory broth along with an assortment of vegetables and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Dragon Meat
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Sear dragon meat until browned on all sides
  2. Saute onions, carrots, celery and garlic in the same pot until tender and fragrant
  3. Add the dragon meat back to the pot along with tomato paste and desired seasonings
  4. Bring mixture back to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for at least 2 hours
  5. Enjoy with a hunk of bread
