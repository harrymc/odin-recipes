Eagle Pie

Eagle pie is a savory dish that typically consists of a filling made of eagle meat and vegetables, such as carrots, peas, and onions, cooked in a creamy sauce, and baked in a pie crust until golden brown. The pie crust can be either a traditional shortcrust pastry or a puff pastry, and is usually made with butter or lard to achieve a flaky, tender texture.

Ingredients

  • Eagle meat
  • Frozen peas
  • Carrots
  • Puff pastry

Steps

  1. Sear eagle meat until browned on all sides
  2. Cook peas and carrots
  3. Put meat, peas and carrots into dish
  4. Cover mixture with puff pastry
  5. Place in over at 200C for 30 minutes
